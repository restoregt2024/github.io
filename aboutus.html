Ryan Smarszcz - Treasurer

Ryan Smarszcz was born in Michigan and spent most of his adult life in Michigan. A 2001 graduate, Ryan acquired a Bachelor of Science from Hope College. Ryan and his wife Stephanie, currently live in Georgetown Township while raising 2 boys.

Ryan has a background in science, statistics, sales, and finance, including tax preparation and treasurer experience within an HOA. After looking to become more involved in the community, Ryan decided that civic duty and using his God given abilities in finance and data, is the best use of his talents and passion.
