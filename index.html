Our Mission

Fiscal Responsibility

Emphasizing the importance of balanced budgets, efficient government spending, and low taxes to foster a healthy economic environment.

Limited Government

Advocating for a government that plays a minimal role in the day-to-day lives of citizens, focusing instead on essential services and enabling personal freedom.

Rule of Law

Upholding the Constitution and the laws of the land, ensuring that justice is administered fairly and without bias.

Public Safety

Prioritizing the safety and security of the community through robust public safety measures and support for law enforcement.

Family Values

Supporting policies that strengthen family units and create supportive environments for raising children.

Personal Responsibility

Encouraging individuals to take responsibility for their actions and decisions as a cornerstone of self-sufficiency and community health.

Free Enterprise

Championing the free market system as the best means to promote economic growth, innovation, and job creation.

Educational Freedom

Supporting the rights of parents to choose the best educational settings for their children, whether it's public, private, or home schooling.

Property Rights

Safeguarding the right to own and use property without excessive government interference.

Patriotism and National Pride

Fostering a sense of pride in the nation's heritage and promoting respect for its symbols and traditions.

Our Campaign

Pro Fiscal Responsibility aka. Fiduciary Duty

Eliminate wasteful spending.

Reduction of water and sewer rates.

Reduce the township millage rate.

Require multiple quotes prior to voting on contracts.

Requiring public auction when township assets are sold.

Pro Transparency

Record all board meetings and sub-committee meetings.

Requiring written minutes on the website.

Start broadcasting live township meetings.

Pro Small Business & Farmers

Your business belongs to you and government regulation should be limited.

Helping farmers with regenerative farming methods, creates healthy land and reliable local food.

Pro Integrity

We understand that the value of honesty and integrity are essential to leadership.

Strong moral character is characteristic to good leadership.

Pro Individual Liberties

Upholding the Constitution, which reflects the principles of good government.

God given inalienable rights are required to be respected and protected by all government.

Pro Limited Government

Just like individual liberties, property rights are important to respect and uphold.

We desire to safeguard and preserve every residents American dream for their future and family.
