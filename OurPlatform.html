Our Platform

Pro Fiscal Responsibility aka. Fiduciary Duty

Eliminate wasteful spending.

Reduce water and sewer rates.

Reduce the township millage rate.

Require bids from multiple vendors on every project prior to voting on contracts.

Require public auction when township assets are sold.

Pro Transparency

Record all board meetings and sub-committee meetings; posting them on both the township website and social media platforms.

Require written minutes posted on the township website in an easily accessible location.

Redesign the township website to make it more easily searchable.

Utilize existing equipment to begin broadcasting live township meetings.

Schedule regular townhall meeting to provide more opportunities to hear from township residents.

Pro Small Business & Farmers

Review existing township ordinances to prevent unnecessary barriers for small business success.

Help farmers with regenerative farming methods, which creates healthy land and reliable local food.

Pro Integrity

We understand that the value of honesty and integrity are essential to leadership.

Strong moral character is paramount to good leadership.

Pro Individual Liberties

Uphold the Constitution, which reflects the principles of good government.

God given inalienable rights demand respect and protection by all levels of government.

Pro Limited Government

Just like individual liberties, property rights are important to respect and uphold.

We desire to safeguard and preserve every resident's American dream for their future and family.
